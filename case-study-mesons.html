Mesons

Website Redesign

Duration

January 2025

Tools used

Mesons Technologies needed a complete overhaul of their outdated site. The navigation was unintuitive, the visuals felt dated, and users often struggled to find relevant information about their services.

I led the redesign, starting with competitor research and user journey mapping. My goal was to create a minimal, clean layout that emphasized clarity and forward-thinking design. In Adobe XD and Figma, I built responsive prototypes, tested typography and layouts, and refined interactions based on user feedback.

The final site was developed in Webflow, with smooth transitions, clean animations, and optimized mobile responsiveness. Post-launch, the site saw a 20% drop in bounce rate and a spike in client inquiries. The new design better reflects Mesons' innovation and makes it easier for visitors to explore their offerings.
